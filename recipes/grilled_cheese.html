Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a warm, gooey American staple. Best served warm with tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread or your choice
  • 1 slice of your favorite type of cheese
  • 1 Tbsp of butter

Directions

  1. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-low heat.
  2. While the pan is waring, butter one side of both slices of bread.
  3. Once the pan is warm, put one slice of bread, butter side down, on the pan.
  4. Layer your cheese on top of the bread. Place the remaining slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese.
  5. Grill the sandwich until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, then flip. Continue grilling until the other slice is also golden brown.
  6. Remove from heat, and cut in half before serving.
