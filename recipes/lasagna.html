Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, meat, and seasonings. Often served with garlic bread, salad, and a glass of wine.

Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna noodles (16 noodles)
  • 1 lb hot Italian sausage
  • 2 eggs
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 Tbsp Italian parsley
  • 2 24-oz jars marinara sauce

Directions

  1. Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat until well browned. Drain.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, roughly 8-10 minutes. Drain.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine eggs, Ricotta, 2 cups of the mozzarella, parmesan, and parsley. Stir until well-combined.
  4. In a 13x9 casserole pan, spread 1 cup of marinara. Be sure to coat the whole pan.
  5. Layer as follows:
    • 4 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of cheese mixture, 1/2 of sausage, 1 cup of mozzarella, and 1 cup of marinara.
    • 4 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of cheese mixture, and 1 1/2 cups of marinara.
    • 4 lasagna noodles, remaining cheese mixture, remaining meat, and 1 cup of marinara.
    • 4 lasagna noodles, remaining sauce, and remaining mozzarella.
  6. Cover in foil and bake in oven for 60 minutes.
  7. Uncover and cook until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  8. Let cool before serving.
