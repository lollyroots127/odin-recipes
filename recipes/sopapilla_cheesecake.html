Sopapilla Cheesecake

Description

A twist on the classic Mexican pastry dessert. A creamy, sweet filling with a flaky sweet crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans Crescent rolls (8 count)
  • 2 8 oz packages of cream cheese, softened
  • 1.5 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 0.5 cup butter, melted
  • 1 Tbsp ground cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350.
  2. Unroll 1 can of crescent dough. Stretch to fit in bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish.
  3. In medium bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 cup of sugar with a mixer until smooth. Mix in vanilla.
  4. Spread cream cheese mixture over dough in the baking dish.
  5. Unroll the second can of dough. Carefully stretch to cover the entire cream cheese layer.
  6. Mix the remaining 1/2 cup of sugar with the cinnamon.
  7. Pour the melted butter evenly over the top of the dough. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar mixture on top.
  8. Bake 30-35 minutes until set. Cool before serving. Store in refrigerator.
